Challenge: Lore of the world - The beginnings

Category: OSINT

Challenge Description
Given a Minecraft screenshot of an elaborate build, we need to identify the "odd moustachioed man" who built it approximately half a year ago, and find the name of his starter base in the same world.
Solution
Step 1: Identify the Builder
The key clue "odd moustachioed man" in the Minecraft community points to Mumbo Jumbo, a popular YouTuber known for his distinctive mustache avatar.
Step 2: Identify the World
The timeframe "approximately half a year ago" and "his best build yet" led to his video titled "MY BEST BUILD" uploaded on December 15, 2024. This build is from Hermitcraft Season 10.
Step 3: Find the Starter Base
Research into Hermitcraft Season 10 revealed that Mumbo Jumbo's starter base was called "the Mothball" - a spherical structure he built at the beginning of the season.
