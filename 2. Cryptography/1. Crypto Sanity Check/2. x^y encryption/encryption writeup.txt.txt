The challenge provides a hex-encoded ciphertext and a Python encryption script (encryptor.py). Inspecting the script reveals it utilizes a symmetric XOR cipher with a hardcoded key, "cybergame". Because the XOR operation ($\oplus$) is inherently its own inverse, running the exact same operation with the ciphertext and the repeating key decrypts the message back to its plaintext format.
