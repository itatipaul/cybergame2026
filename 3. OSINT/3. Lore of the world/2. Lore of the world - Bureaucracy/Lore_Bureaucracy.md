Challenge Description
Given a screenshot of an interior building from the same Hermitcraft Season 10 world, we need to identify a building that represents hated bureaucracy where people tried to fill documents but mostly failed due to messy processes.
Solution
Step 1: Identify the Building Context
The clues point to a bureaucracy-themed building in Hermitcraft Season 10:

"Most hated" for what it represents
People had to fill in documents
Messy bureaucracy caused most to fail

Step 2: Research Hermitcraft S10 Bureaucracy
Searching for "Hermitcraft Season 10 bureaucracy permit document" revealed the Permit Office - a building created by Grian that became notorious for its annoying bureaucratic processes.
Step 3: Find the Document Name
The Permit Office required Hermits to fill out Form MJYAAFK06 - a deliberately confusing document with nonsensical questions like the "Base Blooben Test" that made the bureaucracy frustrating and caused many to fail or give up.
